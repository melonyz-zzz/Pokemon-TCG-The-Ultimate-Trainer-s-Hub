Turn Flow: What You Can Do

A Pokémon TCG match is played in turns. On your turn, follow these steps in order:

Step 1: Start
Draw a Card

You MUST draw a card from your deck at the beginning of your turn. If you can't, you lose the game.

Step 2: Actions
Main Phase
  • Put Basic Pokémon on the Bench.
  • Evolve your Pokémon.
  • Attach ONE Energy card.
  • Play Trainer cards (Items, Supporters).
Step 3: Combat
Attack

Check if your Active Pokémon has enough Energy. Declare an attack, apply damage, and then your turn immediately ends.

Step 4: End
Pass Turn

After you attack (or if you choose to pass without attacking), it is now your opponent's turn.

Core Mechanics

Energy Cost

Attach matching Energy cards. Only ONE per turn.

Weakness & Resistance

Weakness (x2 damage), Resistance (-30 damage).

Evolution Stages

Basic -> Stage 1 -> Stage 2. Cannot evolve on first turn.
